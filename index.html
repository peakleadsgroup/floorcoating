What's included in your lifetime warranty?
Our lifetime warranty covers delamination, peeling, cracking, yellowing, and fading for as long as you own your property. If your coating fails due to material defect or installation error, we'll repair or replace it at no cost. This doesn't cover damage from abuse or improper maintenance, but normal wear and tear is fully covered.
How do I prepare my floor before you arrive?
Simpleâ€”just clear the space. Remove vehicles, storage items, and anything on the floor. Our crew handles all the technical preparation including cleaning, grinding, crack repair, and surface profiling. We'll provide a detailed preparation checklist when you schedule your project.

Polyaspartic coatings are the superior choice for several reasons: they're 4x stronger, cure dramatically faster, won't yellow in sunlight, resist hot tire pickup, and perform in extreme temperatures.
